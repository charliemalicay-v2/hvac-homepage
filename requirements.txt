## 🏠 What to build

A complete, responsive homepage for **Desert Peak Heating & Cooling**, an HVAC company in **Henderson, Las Vegas**.

Content

Design System

<aside>
⚠️

We're looking for something that feels like a real design studio made it - **not** a generic AI-generated site.

</aside>

## 🤖 Use AI for it

Please build this with AI tools - **Claude Code** is our preference. The role is all about developing websites with AI, so we mostly want to see how you drive the tools: how you prompt them, where you step in, and how you get them past their default look.

AI should do plenty of the work; it just shouldn't *look* like it did.

## ✅ What we care about

- A faithful, polished translation of the provided design system into a real page
- Clean, responsive code that looks great on mobile, tablet, and desktop
- Attention to detail - typography, spacing, and hierarchy that match the design intent
- Good performance - optimized images, nothing bloated

## ⚙️ Tech stack

Pick either **Astro** or **Next.js**, and tell us why in the README. We're interested in your reasoning more than the choice itself - things like static vs. server rendering, performance for a mostly-static marketing site, build complexity, SEO, and hosting.

<aside>
💡

There's no wrong answer here - we're judging the thinking, not the pick.

</aside>

## ⭐ Bonus points

Hook the content up to a headless CMS (Sanity, Storyblok, Payload, WordPress headless - your call) so it's editable without touching code.

## 📬 What to send us

- A live link
- The code repo
- A short README covering: why you chose your stack and how you used AI (which tools, what you prompted, where you jumped in). Just a quick explanation of your process.

## 🚀 Hosting

You can deploy free on **Vercel**, **Netlify**, or **Cloudflare Pages** — any of them works for both stacks. If you go with Astro, **GitHub Pages** is an option too.
